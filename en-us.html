Welcome to this new project presented by Edgewater 28 LLC. We are a company that seeks the opportunity and vision of growth. Miami is today an important business center, and one of the cities with the fastest population growth in the United States, which is causing an unprecedented real estate boom. In 2019, through various market analyses, we detected a city sector that was becoming a center for art, design, and nightlife: the Design district - Edgewater - Wynwood area.

In our desire to grow, we started looking for a piece of land to start our project and after several months of negotiation, we became the owners of 15,000 sqft at 329/337/339 NE 28 St, in the heart of Edgewater, steps away from Biscayne Boulevard, and a block away from Biscayne Bay.

The area features a rich atmosphere with restaurants, art galleries, bike shops, and flower shops. The focus is on PRACTICALITY, GOOD LOCATION AND DESIGN. The collaboration between architecture firm OPAa from Argentina and Caymares Martin from Miami resulted in an 8-story, 110,000-square-foot building. It will have 53 apartments and 76 parking spaces, and various amenities such as a swimming pool and a gym.

LOCATION

Design Disctrict

The arts and entertainment district is an urban residential neighborhood with many high-rise residential towers as well as some smaller-scale historic buildings. It is the home of the Adrienne Arsht Center for the Performing Arts, the former home of the Miami Herald and the Omni International Mall.

Miami's Art Disctrict is an innovative neighborhood with access to world-class cuisine, shopping, art, events. The Art Disctrit is a retail hall and includes over 170 brands, including premium stores, making it the ultimate destination for fashion enthusiasts.

Brickell

Miami is synonymous with beautiful beaches and towering palm trees, but in its urban center, Brickell, is the financial hub of Miami known as the “Manhattan of the South” of Miami. Modern and towering high-rise buildings dominate its skyline by Biscayne Bay. It's also a trendy neighbourhood, offering world-class hotels and some of the city's best shopping and nightlife scenes.

This financial center serves as headquarters for more than 1,400 multinationals such as AIG, American Airlines, Cisco, Disney, Exxon, FedEx, Kraft Foods, Microsoft, Oracle, SBC Communications, Sony and Visa International, among others.

Wynwood

Wynwood is currently the artistic heart of the city, one of the most recommended areas to visit in Miami. The streets of this neighborhood have been decorated with colorful murals and graffiti, most of it painted by some of the most important street art artists in the world. it has more than 200 murals, more than any other place on the planet. In recent years it has undergone a great urban and cultural development, to become the epicenter of street art in the United States. The area is home to trendy restaurants, bars and cafes, craft breweries, and plenty of art galleries. Also, we cannot forget the two most famous attractions to see in Wynwood: the Design District and the Wynwood Walls gallery.

Little Havana

Little Havana - the heart of Miami's Cuban diaspora - is part of what makes Miami such a dynamic cultural destination. The neighborhood centers around Calle Ocho (Southwest Eighth Street), a vibrant street lined with Latin-inspired restaurants, bakeries, fruit stands, cigar shops, rum bars, art galleries, and music venues.

Miami Beach

Miami Beach is a global destination that offers far beyond sun and sand, encompassing world-famous nightlife and world-renowned art galleries, first-class hotels and second-to-none dining, designer shopping and a unique architectural style that makes it one of the world's most visually distinctive regions. there is a extensive variety of hotels to stay and enjoy a visit to this iconic city.

Bayside Marketplace

The Bayside Marketplace offers the ideal mix between Shopping Center, outdoor food market and starting point for some of the best tourist activities in the city. Many tourist boats depart from this point and the famous Hop On Hop Off buses have one of their stops here. THERE ARE restaurants for all tastes as well as bars to have cocktails and enjoy the views.
